A Murder Puzzle

This study examines the link between personality and how people respond to moral dilemmas.

You’ll be presented with 3 moral dilemmas. After each dilemma you will take a short, validated personality scale. There are no right or wrong answers — please just answer honestly.

All responses are anonymous.

On desktop, you can press Enter to move to the next question.
